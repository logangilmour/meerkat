So, Here’s the Problem…

Governments need to get on the internet to work better with the people they serve.

Governments need to live up to privacy, accessibility, transparency and other democratic standards.

Before, this has kept government safe — but closed away.

But don’t worry. We’ve solved it.

Now, it’s possible for governments to work in the open, across siloes, with partners.

Get access to the government data you need - whichever government hosts it

Find data and info that is currently fragmented in government silos — tagged by subject, open in useful formats — and see who else is working with it.

Project Meerkat hosts government info & data that is up-to-date, open, searchable, connected and usable.

Quick, easy ways to search for people you need to get your work done - whichever institution or community they work in

Search functions that cut across institutional siloes

Project Meerkat connects the people inside government creating policies and programs with the people who know what it looks like from outside

Tools to work together. Open and secure.

Share important documents in the open, and keep up the privacy, accessibility, transparency and record keeping democratic protections that will keep them public for the long term

Project Meerkat is a set of open source services built to meet your needs - and keep public protections in place, too
